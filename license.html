license

We work with an extensive network of distributors comprising well-known global companies. However, licensing directly from us offers the best value.

We offer several licensing options:

1. Purchase a show license via a one-time payment. We can offer a good discount for this option.

2. Recognizing that planetarium budgets can be limited ( due to annual funding constraints), we offer installment plans spanning several months or years.

3. Or we can offer revenue sharing as *Ticket Share Model*. Pay a percentage of ticket revenue instead of a fixed upfront fee.

Our shows—particularly those for children—have gained worldwide popularity. They are successfully screened in planetariums across Europe, the USA, China, Japan, and Korea.
